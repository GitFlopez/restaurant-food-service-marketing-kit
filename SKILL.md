## What This Skill Does

Generates complete marketing and customer communication assets for restaurants, cafes, food trucks, catering companies, and food service businesses. Every output enforces FDA menu labeling requirements, FTC endorsement guidelines, TABC/liquor advertising rules (where applicable), and local health permit disclosure standards — so the operator publishes, not revises.

## The 4 Prompts

### Prompt 1 (Free) — Grand Opening & New Customer Acquisition Kit
Generate a complete launch marketing package:
- Google Business Profile description (750 chars, keyword-optimized)
- Grand opening announcement email + SMS campaign
- First-visit offer (not "free" — structured as a bounce-back to comply with state coupon laws)
- Yelp and Google review request sequence (FTC 2023 compliant — no conditional language, no incentives)
- Facebook/Instagram bio + first 5 posts (food photography caption templates + hashtag strategy)
- Press release to local food editors (AP style)
- Health permit and sanitation score display guidance (states that require public display)

### Prompt 2 — Menu Copywriting + Digital Menu Optimization
Write menu copy that drives order value and complies with FDA menu labeling:
- Full menu section rewrites (appetizers, mains, desserts, drinks) with sensory language and upsell framing
- Calorie disclosure implementation guide (FDA requires chains with 20+ locations to post calories; state laws vary for smaller operators)
- "Best seller," "chef's favorite," "locally sourced" label copy (FTC: no unsubstantiated superlatives)
- Limited-time item (LTO) launch copy: menu callout, social post, email blast, in-store signage
- Alcohol menu copy (TABC/ABC compliance: no "free drink" offers, no promoting intoxication, age-gate language for digital menus)
- Allergy disclaimer template (FDA Food Safety Modernization Act — Big 9 allergens)
- Online ordering menu optimization (DoorDash, Uber Eats, Grubhub title + description character limits enforced)

### Prompt 3 — Customer Retention & Loyalty System
Build a retention engine without a third-party platform:
- 5-email post-visit sequence (receipt follow-up → review ask → birthday/anniversary → win-back → VIP upgrade)
- SMS loyalty opt-in campaign (TCPA compliant: double opt-in language, opt-out instructions in every message, sender ID)
- Reservation confirmation and reminder sequence (OpenTable, Resy, direct booking)
- Birthday and anniversary program copy (email + SMS + in-store server script)
- Win-back campaign for lapsed customers (90 days no visit — 3-touch sequence)
- Catering inquiry response templates (5 scenarios: corporate lunch, wedding, private event, pickup order, large party inquiry)
- Response templates for 1-star, 3-star, and 5-star reviews (Google + Yelp + TripAdvisor tone differences)

### Prompt 4 — Local SEO + Social + Paid Ads System
Drive discovery and fill seats:
- Google Business Profile post calendar (52 weeks — weekly post prompts by season, holiday, LTO)
- Local SEO landing page copy ("best Italian restaurant Henderson NV" structure — H1, intro, menu highlights, hours, parking, schema FAQ JSON-LD)
- Facebook/Instagram ad copy (3 formats: awareness, offer, retargeting; Meta character limits enforced; no "diet" claims without FDA substantiation)
- Google RSA ad copy (15 headlines 30 chars, 4 descriptions 90 chars; no superlatives without qualification)
- Nextdoor neighborhood post template (community tone — not ad-copy tone)
- Food influencer outreach email (FTC 2023: compensation disclosure brief, no "honest review" language)
- Event promotion kit (holiday, trivia night, live music, wine dinner — Facebook event + email + social + press)

## Compliance Built In

Every output enforces:
- **FDA Menu Labeling Rule (21 CFR 101.11):** Calorie disclosure guidance for chains 20+ locations; voluntary disclosure framing for independents
- **FDA Food Safety Modernization Act:** Big 9 allergen disclosure template in menu copy
- **FTC 2023 Endorsement Guides:** No incentivized reviews, no "honest review" language, influencer compensation disclosure
- **TABC / State ABC Rules:** No promoting intoxication, no "drink free," no targeting minors, age-gate language for digital alcohol menus
- **TCPA:** SMS templates include double opt-in, opt-out instruction, sender ID
- **CAN-SPAM:** All email templates include unsubscribe link placeholder and physical address field
- **State Health Permit Display:** Disclosure guidance for states requiring public display of inspection scores (CA, NY, TX, NV among others)
- **FTC Superlatives:** "Best in Las Vegas" / "World's best" blocked — replaced with attributable claims ("Voted Best Burger, Henderson Home & Garden, 2025")
