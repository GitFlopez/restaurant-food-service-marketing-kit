# Marketing Brief: Restaurant & Food Service Marketing Kit

## ClawHub Marketplace Listing Copy

**Title:** Restaurant & Food Service Marketing Kit — Grand Opening, Menu Copy, Retention + Local SEO

**Tagline:** From launch to loyalty — complete marketing system for restaurants, cafes, food trucks, and caterers. Compliance built in at generation time.

**Description (marketplace copy):**

Running a restaurant means wearing every hat — chef, manager, marketer, compliance officer. This skill handles the marketing layer so you can stay in the kitchen.

4 prompts covering the full customer lifecycle:

**Prompt 1 (Free):** Grand opening kit — GBP description, launch emails, SMS campaign, review requests, press release, 5 social posts, health permit display guidance.

**Prompt 2:** Menu copywriting — sensory copy that drives order value, FDA menu labeling guidance by chain size, DoorDash/Uber Eats/Grubhub character-limit optimization, allergen disclaimers, alcohol menu compliance.

**Prompt 3:** Retention system — 5-email post-visit sequence, TCPA-compliant SMS loyalty program, reservation confirmation/reminder sequence, 5 catering inquiry templates, review response templates for 1/3/5-star reviews.

**Prompt 4:** Discovery engine — 12-week GBP post calendar, local SEO landing page copy with schema FAQ JSON-LD, Google RSA ad copy (15 headlines/4 descriptions), Facebook/Instagram ads (awareness + offer + retargeting), Nextdoor community post, food influencer outreach email, event promotion kit.

Every output enforces FDA, FTC 2023, TABC, TCPA, and CAN-SPAM requirements — no lawyer pass needed before publishing.

**Included example:** The Copper Skillet, Henderson NV — complete worked output across all 4 prompts.

## Target Buyer Personas

1. **The Independent Restaurant Owner** — just opened or 1–3 years in, no marketing budget for an agency, writing their own social posts at midnight. Needs a system.
2. **The Restaurant GM** — owner has handed off operations, GM needs to launch marketing programs without a dedicated team.
3. **The Catering Company Owner** — strong word-of-mouth but no digital presence; needs a launch kit to go beyond referrals.
4. **The Food Truck Operator** — multiple locations, rotating schedule, needs location announcements and social posts fast.
5. **The Restaurant Marketing Agency** — manages 5–50 locations; prompt collection saves 4–8 hours per new client onboarding.
