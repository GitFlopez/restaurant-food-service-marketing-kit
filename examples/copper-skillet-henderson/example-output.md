### Press Release

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

**Farm-to-Table American Diner The Copper Skillet Opens in Henderson's Green Valley Ranch on April 30, 2026**

HENDERSON, NV — The Copper Skillet, a scratch-kitchen American diner sourcing produce and proteins from Nevada farms, opens Wednesday, April 30, at 2415 Warm Springs Rd in Henderson's Green Valley Ranch neighborhood.

The restaurant seats 48 and serves breakfast and brunch Tuesday through Sunday, with dinner service Thursday through Saturday evenings. A beer and wine license is in effect for all service periods.

Owner Maria Reyes, a 20-year resident of Henderson, built the menu around Nevada-grown ingredients: eggs from Moapa Valley Family Farm, grass-fed beef from a ranch outside Ely, and seasonal produce from small growers in the Las Vegas valley. The kitchen prepares all stocks, braises, and sauces from scratch — no pre-made bases or commercial shortcuts.

Signature dishes include the Skillet-Braised Short Rib Hash, prepared overnight and finished to order, and the Cast Iron Herb Chicken, air-chilled and finished in seasoned cast iron on the dinner menu. Breakfast plates are priced between $12 and $18; dinner entrees range from $22 to $36.

The Copper Skillet earned a Clark County health inspection score of 96 in its pre-opening inspection (March 2026).

Reservations are available via Resy; walk-ins are welcome.

**About The Copper Skillet**  
The Copper Skillet is an independently owned American diner in Henderson, Nevada, committed to scratch cooking and locally sourced ingredients. Founded in 2026 by Maria Reyes, the restaurant serves breakfast, brunch, and dinner at 2415 Warm Springs Rd, Henderson, NV 89014.
